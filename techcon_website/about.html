History of TechCon

Techcon Consulting is our new chapter. Techcon started in 2011, centered on design and manufacturing. Insight Engineering began its consultancy 2016. In 2023, we joined forces, forming a new consultancy division at Techcon. This new start lets us adapt to today’s demands, giving us a clear edge in the current market.

Mission of TechCon

IBM TechCon 2023 brings together AIOps, IT automation, integration, cloud native development, and application modernization domain expertise. TechCon 2023 will be held virtually March 21-23 as an interactive digital experience with access to the top minds in IBM Automation and IBM Partners.
